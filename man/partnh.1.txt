.TH PARTNH 1 LOCAL

.SH NAME

partnh - Fit and select non-homogeneous models of sequence evolution from a branch clustering tree.

.SH SYNOPSIS

.B partnh [options]

.SH AVAILABILITY

All UNIX flavors

.SH DESCRIPTION

partnh takes as input a clustering tree of branches and use it to define partitions in the phylogenetic tree with distinct substitution models.

.SH OPTIONS

You should refer to 'info testnh' or to the online manual of testnh for a complete list of available options.

.TP 5

--noninteractive

generates output for redirection in a file.

.TP

param=file
